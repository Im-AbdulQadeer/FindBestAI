How We Rate Tools

Every tool on Find Best AI is rated across five dimensions: output quality, ease of use, pricing value, free tier generosity, and active development. Our overall rating is a weighted average of these scores, informed by our own testing and community feedback.

We never accept payment for ratings or rankings. Tools can submit themselves for review via our contact page, but submission does not guarantee a positive review or listing.

Advertising

Find Best AI is free to use and supported by display advertising and occasional affiliate partnerships. Affiliate links are always clearly disclosed. Advertising never influences our editorial ratings or tool rankings.
