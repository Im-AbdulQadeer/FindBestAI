Artificial intelligence (AI) is a wide field in computer science. It focuses on creating systems that can perform tasks. These tasks typically need human intelligence. Such tasks include learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5] ## Core Technologies * Machine Learning (ML): Algorithms allow systems to learn from data. They improve without specific programming. * Natural Language Processing (NLP): This technology enables computers to understand and respond to human language. * Computer Vision: AI can see and identify objects in images or videos. [2, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10] ## Popular AI Tools & Platforms * [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/): A versatile chatbot for writing, coding, and problem-solving. * [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/): Google's assistant for planning, brainstorming, and generative tasks. * [Claude](https://claude.ai/): An AI assistant from Anthropic. It is designed for safety and accuracy in professional workflows. * [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/): An AI-powered search engine. It provides cited answers to complex queries. [11, 12, 13, 14, 15] ## Common Applications * Creative Content: This involves generating images, audio, and videos from text. * Personalized Recommendations: Algorithms suggest movies or products. * Automation: Handling repetitive tasks like data entry or scheduling through AI agents. * Autonomous Systems: This enables self-driving cars to navigate and make decisions.
