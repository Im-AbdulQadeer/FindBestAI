10 Free AI Tools That Replace Expensive Software

You do not need to spend thousands on software subscriptions. These 10 free AI tools can replace some of the most expensive software in the world — and in many cases, they produce comparable or better results for everyday use cases.

📋 The 10 free replacements
  • Remove.bg → Photoshop background removal
  • CapCut → Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Canva AI → Adobe Express / InDesign
  • Grammarly → ProWritingAid
  • NotebookLM → Expensive research tools
  • Codeium → GitHub Copilot
  • Bing Image Creator → Midjourney
  • Gamma → PowerPoint
  • Fathom → Otter.ai Pro
  • ChatGPT → Jasper AI

1. Remove.bg → Replaces Photoshop Background Removal

Background removal in Photoshop used to require 15–20 minutes of careful work with selection tools. Remove.bg does it in 5 seconds, completely free for small images, with edge detection that is genuinely better than what most users can achieve manually in Photoshop.

The free plan gives you background-removed images at smaller resolution — perfect for web use. The $9/month plan adds HD downloads and bulk API access. For product photography, e-commerce listings, and headshots, Remove.bg has completely displaced Photoshop in many workflows.

💰 Money saved: Adobe Photoshop costs $20.99/month. Remove.bg is free for most use cases.

2. CapCut → Replaces Adobe Premiere Pro for Social Media

For short-form video editing — the most common video task in 2025 — CapCut is free, faster than Premiere, and better optimised for social media output. Auto captions, background removal, trending templates, AI effects, and text-to-speech are all built in and free.

CapCut does not replace Premiere for feature film editing or complex multi-track work. But for 90% of creators making content for Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts, CapCut is not just good enough — it is actually better suited to the task.

💰 Money saved: Adobe Premiere Pro is $54.99/month. CapCut is free.

3. Canva AI → Replaces Adobe Express & Basic InDesign Use

Canva's free tier is genuinely powerful. With AI image generation, thousands of templates, background removal, and brand tools, it handles the vast majority of design tasks that small businesses and creators need. The free tier does have limits, but the $15/month Pro plan is a fraction of the Adobe Creative Cloud cost.

💰 Money saved: Adobe CC All Apps is $59.99/month. Canva Pro is $15/month or free.

4. Grammarly → Replaces ProWritingAid & Basic Editing

The free version of Grammarly catches approximately 90% of grammar and spelling errors and provides basic tone suggestions. For most writers, this is all they need. Upgrade to Premium only if you need advanced style suggestions, plagiarism detection, or the new generative AI rewrite features.

💰 Money saved: ProWritingAid costs $79/year. Grammarly free is $0.

5. NotebookLM → Replaces Expensive Research Subscriptions

Google's NotebookLM is completely free and lets you upload 50 sources (PDFs, Google Docs, websites, YouTube videos) and have an AI-powered conversation with your research. The Audio Overview feature generates a podcast-style discussion of your sources that is genuinely useful for absorbing complex material.

This replaces tools that cost $30–$100/month for researchers, students, and knowledge workers who need to synthesize large volumes of text.

💰 Money saved: Research tools like Elicit and Consensus charge $10–$30/month. NotebookLM is free.

6. Codeium → Replaces GitHub Copilot

Codeium is the best free alternative to GitHub Copilot. It provides AI code autocomplete in 70+ programming languages across 40+ editors — including VS Code, JetBrains, Vim, and Emacs — completely free for individual developers. Quality is excellent and in our testing, Codeium suggestions were often as good as Copilot's.

💰 Money saved: GitHub Copilot Individual is $10/month. Codeium is free.

7. Bing Image Creator → Replaces Midjourney for Everyday Use

Bing Image Creator gives you unlimited free DALL-E 3 image generation. For social media graphics, blog illustrations, presentation images, and creative exploration, Bing Image Creator produces results that were considered cutting-edge just a year ago — completely free, no account required to try.

💰 Money saved: Midjourney starts at $10/month. Bing Image Creator is free.

8. Gamma → Replaces PowerPoint for Modern Presentations

Gamma generates complete, beautifully designed presentations from a single text prompt in under 60 seconds. The free plan allows unlimited AI generations. If you spend time making slide decks and are not a designer, Gamma will produce better results faster than anything you could create in PowerPoint.

💰 Money saved: Microsoft 365 Personal is $6.99/month. Gamma is free for core features.

9. Fathom → Replaces Otter.ai Pro

Fathom records, transcribes, and summarizes Zoom calls with no limits — completely free. Action items, key topics, and follow-up email drafts are generated automatically. It also syncs highlights to Salesforce, HubSpot, and Notion. Otter.ai's comparable features cost $16.99/month.

💰 Money saved: Otter.ai Pro is $16.99/month. Fathom is free.

10. ChatGPT (Free) → Replaces Jasper AI & Copy.ai

ChatGPT's free tier, now powered by GPT-4o, can write blog posts, marketing copy, product descriptions, social captions, and email sequences — all the things that Jasper AI ($49/month) and Copy.ai ($49/month) charge premium prices for. The main thing paid writing AI tools add is templates and workflow structure, which most solo users do not need.

💰 Money saved: Jasper AI starts at $49/month. ChatGPT free is $0.

Total Potential Savings

If you were paying for all the tools these replace: Photoshop ($21) + Premiere ($55) + Adobe CC ($60) + ProWritingAid ($7) + Research tools ($20) + Copilot ($10) + Midjourney ($10) + Microsoft 365 ($7) + Otter.ai ($17) + Jasper ($49) = approximately $256/month or $3,072/year. These 10 free AI tools replace all of it.
