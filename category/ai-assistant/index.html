Frequently Asked Questions

01
What is the best AI assistant in 2026?
▾
ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), and Gemini (Google) are the top three AI assistants in 2026 based on real user ratings across 120,000+ reviews. ChatGPT leads in breadth of features, plugin integrations, and voice mode capabilities. Claude stands out for nuanced reasoning, extremely long documents (200K token context), and coding tasks. Gemini is the top completely free option with real-time Google Search integration and deep Workspace support. For most users, Claude or ChatGPT will be the best choice — both offer powerful free tiers.
02
Which AI assistant is completely free to use in 2026?
▾
Eight AI assistants are completely free with no credit card required: Gemini (Google), DeepSeek, Llama (Meta AI), Gemma (Google), Phi-4 (Microsoft), Pi (Inflection), Qwen (Alibaba), and Kimi (Moonshot AI). ChatGPT and Claude also offer generous free tiers with daily limits on their most powerful models. DeepSeek is particularly impressive — it rivals GPT-4 quality while being completely free, making it the best free AI assistant for technical and coding tasks.
03
What is the best free ChatGPT alternative?
▾
The four best free ChatGPT alternatives in 2026 are: Claude (free tier — best for reasoning and documents), Gemini (completely free — best with Google apps), DeepSeek (completely free — best for coding), and Grok (limited free at grok.com — real-time Twitter/X data). For general use, Claude's free tier is the closest match in quality to ChatGPT Plus. For Google users, Gemini is the obvious choice since it integrates directly with Gmail, Docs, and Drive.
04
Which AI assistant is best for coding and programming?
▾
Claude is the top-rated AI assistant for coding in 2026, particularly for complex projects. Its 200K token context window lets you feed in entire codebases for analysis and refactoring. Claude Code (the dedicated coding tool) takes this further with agentic file editing capabilities. DeepSeek is the best completely free coding assistant — it was specifically trained on code and rivals paid models. For quick code snippets and debugging, ChatGPT and Gemini are excellent free options as well.
05
ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini — which should I choose?
▾
Here's the simple breakdown: Choose ChatGPT if you want the most feature-rich assistant — it has image generation (DALL-E), voice mode, the widest plugin ecosystem, and is the most versatile for everyday tasks. Choose Claude if you work with long documents, need precise coding help, or want the most thoughtful, nuanced responses — it has the largest context window and is rated #1 for reasoning. Choose Gemini if you live in Google Workspace (Gmail, Docs, Drive, Calendar integration) or need a completely free, capable AI with real-time web search built in.
