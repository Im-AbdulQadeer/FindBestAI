AI Assistants — FAQ

What is the best AI assistant in 2026? ▾
ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are consistently rated the top 3 AI assistants in 2026. ChatGPT (OpenAI) leads in breadth of features and integrations; Claude (Anthropic) excels in reasoning, long documents, and coding; Gemini (Google) integrates best with Google Workspace and is completely free.
Which AI assistant is completely free? ▾
Gemini, DeepSeek, Llama (Meta AI), Gemma (Google), and Phi-4 (Microsoft) are completely free AI assistants with no credit card required. ChatGPT and Claude also offer generous free tiers with unlimited basic usage.
What is the best free alternative to ChatGPT? ▾
The top free alternatives to ChatGPT are Claude (free tier with powerful reasoning), Gemini (completely free, great for Google users), and DeepSeek (free, excellent for coding and technical tasks). All three offer comparable or better quality for everyday use.
Which AI assistant is best for coding? ▾
Claude and DeepSeek are widely considered the best AI assistants for coding in 2026. Claude Code is purpose-built for developers with agentic coding capabilities. DeepSeek Coder excels at complex algorithms and is completely free. Grok 3 from xAI is also highly rated for technical tasks.
ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini — which should I use? ▾
Choose ChatGPT for the widest range of features, plugins, and integrations. Choose Claude for long documents, nuanced reasoning, and coding tasks — it has the largest context window. Choose Gemini if you're in the Google ecosystem (Gmail, Docs, Drive) or need a completely free option without limits. All three are excellent for everyday tasks.
