// FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What is the best AI assistant in 2026? ▾
ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are consistently the top 3 AI assistants in 2026. ChatGPT (OpenAI) leads in breadth of features and plugin integrations. Claude (Anthropic) excels in nuanced reasoning, long documents, and coding. Gemini (Google) integrates deeply with Google Workspace and is completely free with no limits.
Which AI assistant is completely free? ▾
Gemini, DeepSeek, Llama (Meta AI), Gemma (Google), and Phi-4 (Microsoft) are completely free AI assistants with no credit card required. ChatGPT and Claude also offer generous free tiers with daily limits on the most powerful models.
What is the best free alternative to ChatGPT? ▾
The top free ChatGPT alternatives in 2026 are Claude (free tier — powerful reasoning), Gemini (completely free, great for Google users), DeepSeek (free, excellent for coding), and Perplexity AI (free with real-time web search). All four offer comparable or superior quality for specific use cases.
Which AI assistant is best for coding? ▾
Claude and DeepSeek are the best AI assistants for coding in 2026. Claude Code is purpose-built for developers with agentic capabilities and a massive 200K context window. DeepSeek is completely free and trained specifically on code. Grok 3 from xAI is also excellent for technical problem-solving.
ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini — which should I choose? ▾
Choose ChatGPT for the widest plugin ecosystem, image generation (DALL-E), and voice mode. Choose Claude for long documents (200K context), nuanced analysis, and coding — it's the most "thoughtful" assistant. Choose Gemini if you live in Google Workspace (Gmail, Docs, Drive integration) or want a completely free, capable AI with real-time Google Search access.
